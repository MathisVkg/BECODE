SEND NOODS is a small company created in 2018 in Brussels. Today, we are present in 2 cities in Belgium and in Luxembourg.

We serve authentic dishes with a modern twist, using a variety home-made noodles and organic ingredients.

Our meals can also be savored as take out or delivered to your doorstep, in their eco-friendly package.

We look forward to seeing you at our table!
